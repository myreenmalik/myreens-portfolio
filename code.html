CRIMINAL JUSTICE AI BIAS
Reducing Racial Disparities in Recidivism Risk Models
Jul 2022 – Aug 2022
PythonNaive BayesRandom ForestAlgorithmic Fairness

Analyzed Naive Bayes and RandomForestClassifier models predicting criminal recidivism, identifying racial bias with disproportionately higher false positive rates for Black defendants. Presented findings on algorithmic fairness and its real-world applications in criminal justice systems.
